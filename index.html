What actually is Cryptored

Mision

This is the begining of Cryptored Labs. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses to understand, create, and manage sustainable NFT projects through education, consultation, and innovative technology.


Vision

We envision a world where NFTs are widely understood, accessible, and utilized as a means of value exchange and ownership representation in a sustainable and equitable manner. We strive to be the leading platform for NFT education and project development, fostering a community of creators and collectors that values innovation, creativity, and social responsibility.

Whats on our mind right now?

These are not in any especific order:
  • Develop a comprehensive educational program: we plan to develop an educational program that covers all aspects of NFTs, from what they are, how they work, how to create them, and how to sell them. The educational program can be offered in the form of online courses, webinars, or workshops.
  • Build a community: One of the most importants for us is to create a community of NFT enthusiasts and experts where people can share knowledge, ask questions, and network. This community can be built through social media, forums, or our dedicated online platforms.
  • Offer consulting services: We also plan to offer consulting services to individuals and companies that want to create NFT projects. This can include advice on design, coding, marketing, and sales.
